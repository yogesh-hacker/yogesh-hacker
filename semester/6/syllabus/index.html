Section A :

  • Marxism: Genealogy, definition, scope in textual reading, major theorists, key terms – Class, Base and Superstructure, Dialectics, Interpellation.
  • Poststructuralism: Genealogy, definition, scope, major theorists, key terms – Logocentrism, Binaries, Deconstruction, Hyperreal-Simulation.

Section B :

  • Feminism: Genealogy, definition, scope in textual reading, major theorists, key terms – Phallocentrism, Androgyny, Sex and Gender, Écriture Féminine.
  • Postcolonial Studies: Genealogy, definition, scope in textual reading, major theorists, key terms (any four) – Imperialism and Colonialism, Orientalism, Nation and Nationalism, Diaspora.
