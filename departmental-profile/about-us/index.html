About Us

Discover the mission and values of the English Department.

Department Overview

The English Department is committed to developing critical thinking, effective communication, and a deep appreciation for literature and language. We offer a diverse curriculum that spans various genres and periods, encouraging students to explore the richness of English literature while honing their analytical skills.

Our Vision

From Paddy to Poetry - explanation - The vision of the department as a literally grass root academic organism is to start from the humble paddy that is cultivated all around and help the students reach the depths of more humbler poetry and poetics of humanities and aesthetics and aspire for more.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in teaching and scholarship.
  • Creativity: Encouraging innovative thinking and expression in literature and language.
  • Community: Fostering a supportive environment that values collaboration and diversity.
  • Respect: Honoring the diverse voices and perspectives within our literary traditions.
